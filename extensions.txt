Extensions:
Sound - 
	Recorded custom sound effects for the ship lasers (3 types), alien death (2 types), and ship death (1 type)
	Included a mute function when you press 'm'

Multiple Waves -
	Allowed a new wave to be created after all the aliens were destoyed, new wave has increased speed

Score-
	Score based off of killing each alien, with bottom aliens being worth more points than top aliens
	Points carry over to a new wave
